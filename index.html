Introduction

As waste management operations all over the world attempt to become cleaner and greener, Pune Municipal Corporation is leading the way with complete end-to-end automation and monitoring of waste collection and management. PMC is using cutting edge technologies like GPS, GSM, RFID, M2M, IOT Sensors along with innovative Mobile and web application to improve and smoothen ground-level mechanisms for waste collection and efficient processing and re-cycling of waste.

Citizen Console
  • Garbage Segregation into three (i.e mixed, dry, wet)
  • Ward wise distributed collection
  • Live GPS tracking of vehicles
Daily Collections
  • Total houses covered daily
  • Total weight of collected garbage
  • Monthly and weekly monitoring of Database
Dashboard
  • Graphical representation of data
  • Pie-charts & Bar-charts to depict garbage distribution
  • Admin privileges to manipulate data

Features

  • Monitoring of the vehicle in Real Time to improve vehicle productivity.
  • Usage and route planning optimization of garbage trucks to make fuel consumption efficient.
  • Alarming system for notifying vehicle break down.
  • Centralized controling and management center for garbage collection and transportation.
  • Efficient monitoring and management of garbage bins.
  • Automated markup and storage of houses and loads of garbage onto cloud.
